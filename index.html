About

Ghost (formerly Ghost B.C. in the U.S.) is a rock band from Linköping, Sweden formed around 2006. The band currently consists of the recently announced Papa Emeritus IV (real name is Tobias Forge) and his band of Nameless Ghouls. Currently they have released four albums and two EPs that are described to be a mix of 70's arena rock riffs and catchy pop melodies intertwined with lyrics involving the dark occult and other nefarious forces. They have gained much notoriety and controversy due to their lyrical content and visual nature of their shows.
